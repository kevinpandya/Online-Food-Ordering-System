About Us

It was the summer of 2020 when we all decided to open a pizzeria. With mouth-watering pizzas prepared by the founders themselves, Hunger's Zone will soon
become the most popular neighbourhood restaurant. Hunger's Zone is about much more than pizzas. From freshly sauteed pastas & delicious appetizers to mocktails, desserts, soups and salads, we have a wide range for you to feast on. All this, combined with the warm, inviting ambience and friendly service, will lead to endless conversations, laughter and memories that you'll cherish forever. So, let your hair down and feel at ease. Go ahead and enjoy the All New Pizza-Xpress experience!
